Places

These maps show some of the places that mean most to me


Home

This is the first place I've ever called home. This is where I grew up with my mom, grandma, grandpa, and (eventually) my younger brother.


Coney Island

Coney Island was one of my favorite places to go to as a kid. My mom would take me there to ride the Cyclone and eat food from Nathan's Famous. I haven't been here in such a long time, and post- COVID-19, I will be going back for the first time with my friends.


Silliman College

Silliman is one of the 14 colleges in the residential college system at Yale. More than that, it has become my home since going awat for college. So many of my favorite memories from the past two years occurred within its walls. These include simply eating with my friends in the dinning hall and laying out in the grassy courtyard on gorgeous weather days. If I could go back in time somehow and choose my residential college again, I would always pick Silliman.
