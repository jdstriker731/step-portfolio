Yale in London

This past summer, I had the opportunity to study abroad in London. I participated in the "Yale in London" summer session, a program that allowed me to take two Yale courses through the Paul Mellon Centre. Not only was I able to get two humanities credits, this trip also let me experience life in the UK. I got to see so many historical landmarks and cultural markets, visit art gallaries (and I saw an original Picasso plastered into a kitchen wall!), and meet so many wonderful people. This was the trip that helped me discover my love for traveling. Now I can't wait to go back to London in the future!
